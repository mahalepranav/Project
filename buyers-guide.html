Getting Started

For those who are yet to buy a bike or scooter and those who need more information about the buying process and dealership services, this page is for you. It is a purchase guide which shares various steps from purchase evaluation to delivery. Feel free to drop by your nearest Yamaha Dealership where our staff would gladly provide their full support in guiding you through this journey.

Step - 1

Checking interested models on Yamaha Website

On our Website, you can check our unique and stylish products to your heart's content. Along with our large variety of products, you would be able to find related content on the design, functionality and technical aspects. Once you find a product of your preference, head down to your nearest Yamaha dealer.

Step - 2

Head down to a Yamaha Dealer

Once you find the model that interests you, head down to your nearest Yamaha Dealership. Awaiting you, are our experts on Yamaha Products. Our Dealer Staff will be glad to answer any concern or questions that you may have.

Step - 3

Check out the Products

At Yamaha Dealership, there is an ambient environment where you can check out our products. Furthermore, you can compare various models at our showroom. If you have any concern, feel free to discuss with our staff who will be sure to help you out.

Step - 4

Test-Ride

You can have a test ride of the models that interest you at Yamaha Dealership. If you feel unsure of handling specific models, rest assured. Our staff shall be glad to assist you. Please experience the thrilling sensation and true power of Yamaha products with your own eyes.

Step - 5

Explanation by Dealer Staff

Before purchase, staff will explain the purchasing, registration methods, loan examination, necessary miscellaneous costs, servicing and maintenance, etc. If you have any concern, feel free to check in with our staff. Furthermore, even without heading down to the dealership, you can reserve certain products online on Yamaha website.

Step - 6

Documentation

Documents required for Vehicle Purchase & Registration are listed below : ID Proof- PAN Card copy (Self attested), Address Proof- Driving License/Election Voter Card/ Aadhar Card/Passport copy with Address page (Utility bills like Electricity bill/ Mobile Bill/ Broadband bill/Rental Agreement are also accepted), 6 Passport size photos- For Finance & RTO processes

Step - 7

Delivery

The various steps before this culminates in the delivery of the product and this marks the step of your Yamaha Life. Customer intimation – of time and date of delivery. Pre-Delivery Inspection done by dealership before customer arrives. Documents collection – invoice, EMI Documents, Warranty, Service schedule, Maintenance tips. Delivery zone – drive check. Check Vehicle Quality, proper document handover to customer

Step - 8

Comprehensive Service

Engine oil, brake kits and batteries are available for bikes & scooters. In addition to that necessary vehicle checks and examinations would take place. To care for your Yamaha Product and safe usage for a long time to come, please send it for regular maintenance. Our Dealers will fully support you even after product delivery. Feel free to discuss with them regarding any concern.

Yamaha Motor made its initial foray into India in 1985 as a joint-venture. In August 2001, it became a 100% subsidiary of Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd, Japan (YMC). In 2008, Mitsui & Co. Ltd. entered into an agreement with YMC to become a joint-investor in India Yamaha Motor Private Limited (IYM).
