Yamaha Motorcycle Connect X

The new “Yamaha Motorcycle Connect X” Application enabled with Bluetooth technology serves a seamless experience of riding and upkeep to the customers in unique ways. The introduction of “Bluetooth enabled technology” aims to bring potential benefits through a host of safety and practical features to the customers.
Answer Back - Upon engaging this feature, the bike indicators start blinking and the horn starts beeping.
Riding History - You can view individual trip details of every ride.
E-Lock - You can lock your bike and prevent theft, using the E-lock feature.
Parking Record - Shows a route map from your current location to where you have parked your bike.
Locate My Bike - Upon clicking this feature, the bike indicators will illuminate for 10 seconds continuously.
Hazard - Upon clicking the button, all the indicators start blinking continuously, to notify fellow riders on the road, to be cautious.
